Games I play

Here's my brief list of my favorite video games I tend to play in my free time. I tend to enjoy them more when playing with and against other on line despite not being a competitive person. I mostly tend to have an interest in games under the genre of "anime fighters" (games like BlazBlue, UNIEL, and Persona) which I picked up from friends. I also play "first person shooters" alot (mainly CoD, but also Halo, and at the moment learning about Destiny)

UNIEL

Under Night In-Birth is a visual novel and 2D fighting game co-developed by Ecole Software and French-Bread, known for the Melty Blood series. It features a brand new cast of characters and a story that spins around modern fantasy. Sega released the arcade version in Japan on September 20, 2012.I have been playing this game for a year and a half and I found out about it through some friends during my sophmore year, they're no longer here but I play online competetitively as the character Merkava (the creature in my background picture).

Call of Duty: Black Ops 3

Call of Duty: Black Ops III is a military science fiction first-person shooter video game,developed by Treyarch and published by Activision.] It is the twelfth entry in the Call of Duty series and the sequel to the 2012 video game Call of Duty: Black Ops II. It was released on Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One on November 6, 2015. A feature-limited version developed by Beenox and Mercenary Technology that only supports multiplayer modes was released on PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360. I mainly play this game like the many variations of the Call of duty series with my friends from middle school I grew up playing CoD games with. We usually play Multiplayer and occasionally Zombies together, we are all currently racing over who will be the first to achieve something in the game, such as in my case the heroic armor which are the gold and black outfits for the main specialist you play as in Multiplayer.
